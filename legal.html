Compliance & Ethics

Legal Framework

Transparency and data protection are the foundations of our international operations.

Effective Date: January 2025

Doc ID: IIG-LP-01

1. Privacy & Data Policy

As a technology-driven agricultural firm, IIG Smart Farmers Limited collects specific data to provide high-end AI Risk Assessments. We respect your privacy and align our practices with international data protection standards.

  • Personal Identification: We collect National ID photos and personal details via USSD (*789*7777#) and our web portal solely for KYC (Know Your Customer) verification.
  • Contact Information: Email addresses and phone numbers are used to communicate loan status and fertilizer distribution schedules.
  • Agricultural Data: Soil health data and farm locations are used to improve our AI models and provide better biotech consultancy.

We do not sell your data to third parties. All sensitive documents are encrypted and stored in secure digital vaults with limited administrative access.

2. Terms of Service

By registering as a member of IIG Smart Farmers Limited, you agree to the following conditions:

  • Eligibility: Services are currently prioritized for small-scale farmers in marginalized societies.
  • Micro-Credit Agreements: Loan repayment cycles are strictly synchronized with harvest periods. Failure to adhere to group banking standards may affect future credit limits.
  • USSD Usage: Usage of the *789*7777# code implies consent to be contacted by our automated bot for service updates.

3. Biotech & Technical Disclaimer

While our Machine Learning models provide highly accurate risk assessments and weather predictions, agricultural outcomes are subject to environmental variables. IIG Smart Farmers Limited provides guidance as an investment support tool, but final farming decisions remain with the member.

4. Regulatory Compliance

IIG Smart Farmers Limited is transitioning toward registration as an International Private Limited Company. We operate under the direct oversight of our founding Board of Directors and adhere to the financial and agricultural regulations of the regions where we provide services.

For formal legal inquiries, please contact our administrative headquarters.
